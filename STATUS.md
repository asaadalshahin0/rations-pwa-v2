# Rations Status

## Current MVP

Local-first PWA for logging meals, estimating calories/macros from a photo or text, tracking daily goals, reviewing history, and optionally syncing encrypted data by phrase.

## Active Task

Complete: add a streak counter for days where the protein goal is hit and calories stay at or under the calorie goal, with a simple daily promise/check-in button.

## Latest Progress

The Today tab now shows the goal streak, a Promise Today button, and a status line explaining what is needed for today to count. Promised dates are stored locally and included in encrypted sync exports. Local browser testing confirmed a qualifying logged day can be promised and increments the streak.

## Smallest Next Step

Use the app normally and adjust wording only if the promise flow feels too strict.
